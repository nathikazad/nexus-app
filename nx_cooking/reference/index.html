Spicy Garlic Noodles

25 min 2 servings Cooked 2d ago

Ingredients

Udon noodles 200 g
Garlic cloves 3
Chili oil 2 tbsp
Sesame seeds 1 tbsp

Instructions

1. Boil water and cook udon noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. Mince the garlic cloves finely.

3. Heat a pan, add chili oil and minced garlic. Sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.

4. Toss the cooked noodles in the garlic oil until well coated. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve immediately.

Notes

Works great with soba noodles too. Add a fried egg on top for extra protein.
